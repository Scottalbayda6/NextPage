St. Scholastica's Academy-Bacolod
We are a CATHOLIC, BENEDICTINE, FILIPINO educational institution that envisions the formation of Christ-centered, socially-oriented Scholasticans with reverence for God, for persons and all creation, imbued with Christian-Benedictine values and living out our ORA ET LABORA. Inspired by this vision, we commit ourselves to provide a HOLISTIC formation anchored on ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE to form students into SOCIALLY RESPONSIBLE AGENTS OF CHANGE in the transformation of a just and equitable society.
